herkforth Mission

My ultimate goal is to make [some] computers easy to use; to make them so that with reasonable intelligence, diligence and training you can get the computer to do just about anything.

I am accomplishing this goal by writing a programming language/environment from scratch by combining some very effective concepts, some experimental ones that look very promising and some of my own invention.

Many thanks particularly to Chuck Moore, Jeff Fox and Jef Raskin for thinking outside the box, for their countless hours of experimentation, and for taking the time to share.

I plan to accomplish this through the herkforth goals.

-- Jason Woofenden
